Transportation

Application of computer in transportation

The following are the developing role of IT in transportation in the 21st century:
  • Applying information systems and technology in the transportation field
  • Applying system user interfaces (e.g. interactive graphics) as well as data management and sharing
  • Using web technologies in transportation
  • Encouraging the use of common information system and IT semantic and standards in the transportation field
  • Facilitating and monitoring technology transfer as “user advocate” among transportation organizations, vendors and universities
  • Evaluating the impact of computer technologies on transportation organizations, including gains in productivity
“Here and now” application of computers to transportation
  • Car Navigation
  • Positive Train Control
  • Mapping
  • Online Booking
  • Weather Forecast
